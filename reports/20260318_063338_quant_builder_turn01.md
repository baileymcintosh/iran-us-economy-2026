### Chart 2 — TTF Gas: Dual Shock Anatomy
The TTF spike reflected a **compound shock**: Hormuz closure (cutting 20% of global LNG transit) **simultaneous** with Ras Laffan shutdown (removing another 20% of global LNG supply). Asian buyers lost their primary supply source, bidding up spot prices globally; at least 8 LNG tankers diverted from Europe to Asia, amplifying European gas prices. US Henry Hub rose only 7% — the US remained largely insulated due to lack of LNG export dependency.
